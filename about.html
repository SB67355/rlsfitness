About RLS

RLS provides a full range of both gym class and personal training to suit all of our clients needs. We provide morning and evening classes to suit all levels of fitness seven days a week in Westport Co. Mayo.

Our Goals

We are not about the latest fads and trends in fitness, we aim to provide you the client with a fitness regime that will work to meet your individual requirements. Our focus is on the tried and tested methods of diet combined with exercise to provide you with the best chance of meeting your own expectations.

Everyone who steps through our door is looking to exercise for different reasons, being aware of this helps us tailor a package for each client and achieve their best results.
